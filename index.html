My Path

My explorations stem from one belief: Technology must empower the individual. I started with NLP and large language models during my research, fascinated by their power to push boundaries. But a stint in Big Tech taught me the same power could be used to weaken individual judgment.


That pursuit of "digital sovereignty" led me to quantitative investment, where I learned to discover market truths rather than invent narratives. But the high barriers and information black boxes in finance are unfriendly to individual explorers.


Ultimately, I found my place at the intersection of Web3 and AI. Web3 uses code to guarantee transparent rules; AI amplifies individual creativity. Their fusion is turning "empowering individuals" from a slogan into reality.


I am now an independent AI & Web3 Builder. I write smart contracts, build decentralized applications, and explore how AI agents can serve individuals — not platforms.
